Lemon curd

Description

Lemon curd is viscous, liquid sunshine. Luxe and bright in flavour and colour, it's delicious filling a tart, or topping waffles, pancakes, or pavlova.

Ingredients

  • 100 g butter, cubed
  • 1 cup (200 g) sugar (white or caster)
  • 1 Tbsp (20 ml) lemon zest
  • 80 ml lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 2 eggs

Method

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together the eggs and the sugar until it forms a thick, uniform paste.
  2. Whisk in lemon juice and lemon zest.
  3. Add butter, then cook on low to medium heat, stirring frequently.
  4. Once the butter has melted into the egg mixture, continue to cook and stir until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Remove from heat and pour into sterilised jars. Keep the finished curd in the fridge, it can be stored for about a month.
